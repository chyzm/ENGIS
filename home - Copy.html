Our Featured Services

Explore our key land-related services — designed to simplify registration, compliance, and ownership processes for individuals, businesses, and developers.

Search Report

Get detailed insights into the ownership, title history, and legal status of a parcel of land. Essential for due diligence before land transactions.

Ground Rent Bill

Easily access, view, and pay your annual ground rent obligations. Stay compliant and avoid penalties with timely payments.

New Registration

Register a newly acquired land or property with the Enugu State Government. Secure your title documents and establish legal ownership.

Recertification

Renew the certification of your land documents to stay in compliance with current regulations and ensure continued legal recognition.

Regularization

Formalize ownership of unregistered land or resolve irregularities in title documents. A crucial step toward secure land tenure.

Estate Unbundling

Break down large estate titles into smaller, independently registered plots. Ideal for property developers and large estate managers.

Partial Surrender/Merger

Officially release a portion of your land title back to the original grantor, while retaining the remaining portion.

Land Allocation

Apply for government-allocated plots through a transparent, merit-based system. Suitable for residential, commercial, or agricultural use.

📝 New Title Registration

This is the first step toward securing legal ownership of a land or property within the state. This process involves the official documentation of a new land title that has not been previously registered in the government's land registry.

💰 Ground Rent Bill

Ease of processing annual fee payable by land owners to the government for the continued use and occupancy of their allocated land. It is a legal obligation tied to land ownership under leasehold arrangements.

🏡 Regularization

Convert your informal or unapproved land holdings into legally recognized titles within government records. It is designed for individuals or organizations occupying land without formal documentation or allocation.
