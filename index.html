Our Featured Services

Explore our key land-related services â€” designed to simplify registration, compliance, and ownership processes for individuals, businesses, and developers.

Search Report

Get detailed insights into the ownership, title history, and legal status of a parcel of land. Essential for due diligence before land transactions.

Ground Rent Bill

Easily access, view, and pay your annual ground rent obligations. Stay compliant and avoid penalties with timely payments.

New Registration

Register a newly acquired land or property with the Enugu State Government. Secure your title documents and establish legal ownership.

Recertification

Renew the certification of your land documents to stay in compliance with current regulations and ensure continued legal recognition.

Regularization

Formalize ownership of unregistered land or resolve irregularities in title documents. A crucial step toward secure land tenure.

Estate Unbundling

Break down large estate titles into smaller, independently registered plots. Ideal for property developers and large estate managers.

Partial Surrender

Officially release a portion of your land title back to the government or for public use. Needed for re-planning or land swaps.

Land Allocation

Apply for government-allocated plots through a transparent, merit-based system. Suitable for residential, commercial, or agricultural use.
